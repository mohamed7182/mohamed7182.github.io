The Best 10 Games of 2024 You Must Play!

by: Mohamed Alawy


1. Elder Scrolls VI

The long-awaited sequel to Skyrim is finally here. Elder Scrolls VI promises to deliver a rich, expansive world filled with lore, adventure, and endless exploration. Bethesda has revamped the graphics engine, ensuring stunning visuals and an immersive experience that will captivate both new and returning fans.

Elder Scrolls VI on steam

2. Cyberpunk 2078

Following the success and subsequent patches of Cyberpunk 2077, CD Projekt Red returns with Cyberpunk 2078. This sequel expands on the gritty, neon-drenched world of Night City, offering deeper narratives, more player choices, and a host of new cybernetic enhancements to experiment with.

Cyberpunk 2078 on steam

3. Horizon: Forbidden Realms

Guerrilla Games continues the saga of Aloy in Horizon: Forbidden Realms. This open-world adventure takes players to new, uncharted territories filled with robotic creatures and ancient mysteries. Enhanced combat mechanics and a gripping story make this a must-play for action-RPG fans.

Horizon: Forbidden Realms on steam

4. Final Fantasy XVII

Square Enix delivers yet another epic entry in its storied franchise with Final Fantasy XVII. This installment features a blend of classic turn-based combat and real-time action, set in a beautifully crafted world that combines magic and technology. The narrative promises to be one of the series’ best, with unforgettable characters and plot twists.

Final Fantasy XVII on playstation store

5. Starfield

Bethesda’s Starfield takes players to the final frontier in an epic space-faring adventure. As the studio’s first new IP in decades, expectations are sky-high. The game promises vast, procedurally generated planets, deep space exploration, and complex storylines that intertwine with the player's choices.

Starfield on steam

6. God of War: Ragnarok's

Kratos returns in God of War: Ragnarok's, the epic conclusion to the Norse mythology saga. Santa Monica Studio has upped the ante with more brutal combat, stunning visuals, and a narrative that delves deep into themes of fate, sacrifice, and redemption.

God of War: Ragnarok's on playstation store

7. Hitman: World of Assassination

IO Interactive takes the Hitman series to new heights with Hitman: World of Assassination. This latest installment features Agent 47 navigating through an array of intricately designed levels across the globe. With enhanced AI, more sophisticated stealth mechanics, and a captivating storyline, this game promises to be a highlight for stealth-action enthusiasts.

Hitman: World of Assassination steam

8. GTA V

Rockstar Games' GTA V continues to captivate players with its expansive open world, engaging narrative, and unmatched freedom. The enhanced version for 2024 brings cutting-edge graphics, new missions, and additional features that push the boundaries of interactive entertainment even further.

GTA V on steam

9. Rocket League

Psyonix's Rocket League remains a favorite among competitive gamers, and the 2024 updates have taken the high-octane action to new levels. With improved graphics, new arenas, and additional gameplay modes, this car-soccer hybrid continues to offer thrilling matches and a vibrant community.

Rocket League on steam

10. Counter-Strike 2

Valve’s CS2 (Counter-Strike 2) revitalizes the classic tactical shooter with updated graphics, new maps, and refined mechanics. Building on the legacy of its predecessor, CS2 aims to deliver an unparalleled competitive experience, making it a must-play for fans of the FPS genre.

Counter-Strike 2 on steam
